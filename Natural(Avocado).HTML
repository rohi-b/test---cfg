    Nutritional Benefits

  • Avocados arre a great source of monounsaturated fat and vitamin E.
  • They also contain other useful minerals such as Iron, Copper, Potassium, Vitamin B and Folate.
  • Just half and avocado counts as one portion of your five a day.

    Health Benefits

  • The monounsaturated fat that is found in avocados helps protect against heart disease and also leads to lower blood pressure.
  • Avocados also contain Vitamin E and a group of carotenes which are thought to keep the eyes healthy.

    Fun Fact

  • Avocados are poisonous to birds, cats, dogs and basically all pets.

Avocado Face Mask

Click here to find your skin type!

  • Avocado is good for moisturising dry skin it can also help to relieve acne by decreasing the oil levels in your skin. A weekly application can help to balance the oils in your skin.

    You will need:

  • Mashed Avocado - to help replenish moisture
  • Plain Yogurt - for moisture and soothing effects
  • Egg white - temporarily plump skin and destroys acne-causing bacteria>
  • Honey - adds a natural glow and acts as a pore cleanser

    Instructions:

  1. Mix all ingredients in a bowl until all of the ingradients is throughourly mixed.
  2. Pull the hair back away from your face to prevent the mask from getting stuck in it.
  3. Apply to clean, dry skin in an even layer.
  4. Avoid any contact with eyes or lips
  5. Leave the mask on skin for 15 minutes before rinsing off with cold water.
  6. Pat the skin dry and put the remaining mask in a jar
  7. Repeat two to three times per week.
